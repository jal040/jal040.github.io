Whova
As the event planning industry adapted into the virtual space over the past year, Whova evolved rapidly to stay ahead of the game. Whova’s event management system is one of the best in the industry and continues to quickly improve. Whova’s system includes a web based dashboard for event organizers and a mobile and web event app for attendees.

I’ve led the designs for a variety of small to large projects on all three platforms. A few projects that I led the designs for were Artifact Center, Networking Tables, Career Fair, and 1-1 Video Chats.

I’m consistently working to improve our design process, by incorporating user research, usability testing, design reviews, design QA, and after-release analytics.
